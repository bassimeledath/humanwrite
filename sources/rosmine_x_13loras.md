> ReLoRA is such a great trick
>
> You repeatedly train a LoRA, merge it, then train a new LoRA on top of that
>
> Each LoRA is only a low rank update, but a sum of low rank updates can be a full rank update, so you can get better results
>
> For DFT, I used a total of 13
